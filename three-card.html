Three Card

How to Play

Card Values:
Reset Cards: Once a reset card is played, the same player can play another card immediately after.
  • - Resets the value of the last played card. Any value card may be played immediately after.
  • - Removes all previous cards from play. Any value card may be played after.
Normal Cards: (from lowest value to highest value).
Rules:
  • To play cards: select a card from your hand, then select the in the middle of the page.
  • Drawing cards is done automatically after cards have been played.
  1. The user always begins the game.
  2. Players will have 3 or more cards in their hands at all times
  3. Players must play a card(s) greater than or equal to the previously played card. *See card values above*
  4. If a player does not have cards high enough to play, they must pick up all previously played cards.
  5. If a player plays 4 cards of the same value (or plays the 4th and final card on top of the other 3 in a row) all previous cards are removed from play and the player plays again.
